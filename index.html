Teator is here to change the education game.

Top Notch Tutors

Teator recruits tutors from education certificate programs, university education majors, and retired teachers.

Customized Curriculum

Tutors collaborate with math teachers in under-served elementary schools to tailor instruction to established class curriculums.

Personalized Instruction

Each tutor is paired with a small group of learners for online drop-in tutoring sessions.

Cutting Edge Tools

Students get the individualized math instruction they need through video chat, image sharing, and digital whiteboard technologies.

Level Up Your Math

Students unlock badges, avatars, and rewards as they progress through the curriculum.

Metrics that Matter

Teachers receive in depth student progress reports, allowing them to recognize and correct common issues and misunderstandings.

Everybody Wins

Students master math at their own pace and on their own terms. Teachers get the help they need to reinforce the concepts they teach in the classroom.

It starts with 1.

Students who already believe they are not as academically successful as their more affluent peers, will further internalize defeat. Teator provides the focused educational plan students need to bolster skills in math.

Better Math Education = A Better Life

Math achievement in elementary school may seem like not that big of a deal, but in fact correlates to a higher quality of life down the road. Students who struggle in elementary school are more likely to begin using drugs and smoking earlier than successful peers.

The Teator Technique

We pair up educators with under-served classrooms.

Each student is paired up with a Teator Tutor.

Tutors use the students' curriculum to provide tailored academic aid.

Teachers are less stressed, students' skills are bolstered.
